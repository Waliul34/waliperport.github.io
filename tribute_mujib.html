Sheikh Mujibur Rahman

The man who founded Bangladesh

Here's a time line of Mujib's life:

  • 1920 - Born in Tungipara, Gopalganj, Bangladesh.
  • 1942 - Passes his Matriculation from Gopalganj Missionary School.
  • 1947 - Joins the Bengal Muslim League.
  • 1944 - Passes his Intermediate of Arts from Islamia College (now Maulana Azad College).
  • 1947 - Passes BA from Islamia College (now Maulana Azad College).
  • 1963 - Becomes the head of the Awami League
  • 1966 - Starts 6-point movement.
  • 1971 - Declares the independence of Bangladesh.
  • 1975 - Gets killed with his family and personal staff by a group of junior army officers.

"Sheikh Mujib loved the countrymen more than his own life. He always fought for the political, economic and cultural emancipation of people."

-- Tofail Ahmed
